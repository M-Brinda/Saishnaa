Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 6, 2025

Saishnaa IT Solutions ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website https://saishnaa.com, engage with our services, or interact with us in any other way.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal, technical, and usage data including cookies and tracking information.

This includes but is not limited to:

  • Personal identification information (Name, email address, phone number, etc.)
  • Technical data (IP address, browser type, device information)
  • Usage data (pages visited, time spent, navigation paths)
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

2. How We Use Your Information

We use this information to provide services, improve experiences, communicate with you, and comply with legal obligations.

Primary uses include:

  • Delivering and maintaining our services
  • Personalizing user experience
  • Improving our website and services
  • Communicating with users
  • Analyzing usage patterns
  • Ensuring security and preventing fraud

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your data. We may share it with service providers or when legally required.

Information may be shared with:

  • Trusted third-party service providers
  • Business partners (with your consent)
  • Legal authorities when required by law
  • Successors in the event of a merger or acquisition

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security to protect your information.

Our security measures include:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Secure server infrastructure

5. Your Data Protection Rights

You may access, update, delete or restrict the use of your data by contacting us at saishnaateam@gmail.com.

Your rights include:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

6. Third-Party Links

Our site may link to third-party sites, which are not under our control.

We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy and will notify users of significant changes.

Notification methods may include:
